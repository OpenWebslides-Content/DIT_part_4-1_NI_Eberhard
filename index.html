Introduction: a short view back

  • first trials with film techniques at the SAPHIR research reactor until 1995
  • offer to build a dedicated beam line for NI at SINQ
  • 1997: NEUTRA becomes operational as first neutron imaging facility at a spallation source, completely based on digital systems
  • 2006: ICON as a facility for cold neutrons with a micro-tomography position
  • 2008: energy selector implemented at ICON
  • 2009: neutron grating interferometer tests  user device
  • 2010: XTRA (complementary X-rays in NEUTRA)
  • 2011/2012: access to BOA (very cold, polarized neutrons)
  • 2012/2013: „neutron microscope“ as project funded
